# Tethys-class

**Nickname:** The Workhorse  
**Manufacturer:** Meridian Shipworks Consortium  
**Introduced:** 2754  
**In Service:** 31 active vessels  
**Cruise Speed:** 0.055 AU/day

> _The backbone of the network._

## Overview

The most common vessel in the Stellar fleet. More people have crossed the Scatter aboard a Tethys-class vessel than any other ship in service. There are currently 31 active units and the class has been in continuous production since 2754 — with updates and variants, but the same fundamental design philosophy: a ship that can do almost anything reasonably well, at a price that makes regular interplanetary travel commercially viable.

Tethys vessels are optimised for Gravity Assist routing — their fuel systems are designed around the assumption of planetary slingshot trajectories, which makes them significantly more cost-effective on longer routes than a ship that has to burn propellant the entire journey.
