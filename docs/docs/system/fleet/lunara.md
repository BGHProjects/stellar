# Lunara-class

**Nickname:** The Observatory  
**Manufacturer:** Aethon Celestial Design Bureau  
**Introduced:** 2769  
**In Service:** 8 active vessels  
**Cruise Speed:** 0.042 AU/day

> _When the journey is the destination._

## Overview

A premium scenic vessel with maximised viewport ratios, an expanded observatory deck, and passenger capacity reduced in favour of space and atmosphere. The Lunara class was developed after data showed that a significant subset of travellers were choosing longer routes not because they were cheaper, but because they wanted more time in transit. The Lunara formalised that instinct into a product.

Its observation deck runs nearly the full circumference of the vessel — a continuous viewport band that gives unobstructed views in almost every direction simultaneously. Lunara routes are specifically plotted to maximise astronomically interesting passes: through the inner edge of the Scatter, into Vareth's ring plane, past Serrath's terminator line.

## Cabin Classes Available

- Orbit Class

- Helix Class

:::info No Full Cryo
Lunara-class vessels do not offer Full Cryo. If you're booked on a Lunara, you're expected to be awake for at least some of it — that's the product. Cryo Intervals are available for passengers who want time compression with curated waking windows.
:::
