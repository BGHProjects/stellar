# Vareth System

Vareth is a gas giant — uninhabitable, spectacular, and the gravitational anchor of three colonised moons. It sits at 3.2 AU from Taunor Prime, on the near side of the Scatter.

## Vareth

**Type:** Gas giant (Jupiter/Venus hybrid)  
**Status:** Uninhabitable — atmospheric pressure and temperature would destroy any vessel  
**Orbital Period:** 890 days  
**Notable Feature:** Permanent hyperstorm at the southern pole, vivid storm band colouring

Vareth is the most visually dramatic object in the system. Its atmosphere layers in vivid, saturated bands of deep crimson, burnt amber, electric violet, and sickly yellow-green, driven by the competing gravitational pulls of both stars and its large moon family. A permanent hyperstorm the size of a small continent sits at the southern pole — a swirling eye of deep purple surrounded by amber walls.

Vareth's powerful magnetosphere creates a natural radiation shield around several of its moons, making them viable for colonisation despite sitting deep in the mid-system.

## Kalos

**Type:** Moon of Vareth  
**Status:** Colonised — 1 spaceport  
**Population:** ~12 million  
**Gravity:** 0.6g  
**Atmosphere:** Thin — filtration masks required outdoors  
**Orbital Period:** 6 days (tidally locked)

### Overview

The largest and most developed moon in the Vareth system. Kalos smells of sulphur and machine oil. The air is breathable barely — requiring filtration masks outdoors in the industrial zones, though the residential domes maintain clean air standards. Deep-core mining operations, the largest refinery complex in the mid-system, and a frontier energy that attracts workers, traders, and a certain kind of traveller who wants somewhere that still feels raw.

Vareth hangs enormous in the sky — its storm bands shifting colours across the horizon, the permanent hyperstorm a swirling violet eye visible even in daylight.

## Thal

**Type:** Moon of Vareth  
**Status:** Colonised — 1 spaceport  
**Population:** ~340,000  
**Gravity:** 0.5g  
**Atmosphere:** Thin — pressure suits required  
**Orbital Period:** 11 days (tidally locked)

### Overview

A tidally heated moon kept geologically active by Vareth's gravitational flexing. Hundreds of geothermal vent fields across the surface, harnessed for energy production that powers most of the Vareth moon network. A scientific and engineering colony more than a civilian settlement, but with a growing volcanic tourism trade.

When Vareth's magnetosphere interacts with Thal's volcanic emissions, the resulting aurora displays are unlike anything else in the system — curtains of green and violet light rippling across the sky above the steam vents, with Vareth's vast form behind them.

## Mira

**Type:** Moon of Vareth  
**Status:** Colonised — restricted access, permit required  
**Population:** ~800 (research personnel, rotating)  
**Gravity:** 0.3g  
**Atmosphere:** None — vacuum surface  
**Orbital Period:** 19 days (tidally locked)

### Overview

An ice moon with a confirmed subsurface ocean and native microbial life. The most scientifically significant body in the system. The discovery of microbial organisms in Mira's subsurface ocean — simple, chemosynthetic, thriving in the mineral-rich thermal plumes — changed the Taunor system's understanding of itself. These organisms evolved here, independently of all other life in the system.

Access is strictly controlled by interplanetary treaty. Visitors are permitted only to the orbital station — no surface landings except for authorised research vessels operating under strict biosafety protocols.

:::warning Permit Required
Mira requires an interplanetary access permit in addition to a voyage ticket. Permits are checked before docking at Mira Access Point.
:::
